Elevator Pitch

My name is John and I make websites. I don’t know about you, but I’ve always had trouble with studying long periods of time, until I found out the Pomodoro Technique, which is where you study for 25-minute sessions and have 5-minute breaks. After 4 study sessions, you take a 30-minute break.
Although it did help me study for longer, the 25-minute sessions didn’t feel long enough to gain momentum and the 5-minute breaks weren’t long enough to have anything fun. This is when I found the Animedoro Technique, where you study for 60 minutes and then watch an episode of anime (20-minute break). This amount of time allowed me to gain a lot of momentum in my studies and also have a meaningful length break, while also watching my favorite anime.
I tried to find an app that would help me track my study sessions, but these apps would only track pomodoro sessions. This is why I want to make a website that tracks custom study sessions; you can choose the length of your study sessions, short-breaks, long breaks, the number of study sessions until your long breaks, and how many study sessions you’ve done; this website will give you feedback and will track your productivity! Purpose Statement

To provide a service that allows people to be more productiveEveryone strives to be more productive, but most people don’t know how or where to start. This website will provide them the structure they need to be more productive and ensure that productivity is sustainable and motivating as well.
We want users to be informed about the benefits of intermittent study sessions, by providing users scientific literature for their knowledge, and allow users to implement the technique through the in-built tools, such as the timer, and customized study sessions. Allowing users to track their sessions will motivate long-term progress as well.
By providing these tools and information, we are allowing users to achieve their goals, whether that be in their studies, careers, or personal life.

Audience Statement

The main target demographic of this website would be mainly for students of all ages and demographics but, with the customized nature of it, the audience can be anyone who is looking to increase and track their productivity, while also being sustainable as well.

As a student myself and being surrounded by others, students can lack structure and discipline. The website will allow people to give structure to the user’s study/productivity sessions and allowing for sustainable, long-term productivity.

Our demographic will mainly access this via the web app. A mobile application may be implemented at a later date, but a web application allows for universal access across all devices.

Misson Statement

∏

Our mission is to enhance human capital. There is no financial goal with this website; what the website is to be is a tool to allow people to flourish and be at their best and achieve their goals, whether that be personal or career goals. To enhance discipline is to enhance one’s life. As one achieves more goals and is disciplined in doing so, more of their time is freed up for other important aspects of their lives, while also allowing them to reach their potential. By helping individuals achieve more of what they want, the benefits will radiate outwards to those around them. Humans are networked individuals and to help one is to help many.

Persona and Scenario Ideas

Persona #1 (David Andrews)

Age: 19
Occupation: Student
Archetype: Nerd
Personality Type: Introvert, Sensing, Feeling, Judging (ISFJ)

Goals:

  • To cut down on time spent gaming.
  • To complete homework on time.
  • To increase test scores.
Frustrations:

  • Not sure how to increase productivity.
  • Wants to reduce gaming addiction
  • Finds it hard to keep concentration on college tasks

Bio:

David is a young college student who wants to change his life around. He is currently has mediocre grades in all of his courses and finds it easier to procrastinate by playing videos, browsing social media, talking to friends online etc. He wants to be able to increase his productivity and decrease his procrastination time.
